Will Bosi Redefines Limits with New Send

Published on February 10, 2024

In an astonishing display of strength and technique, Will Bosi has achieved a groundbreaking new send on the famed route Burden of Dreams (V17) in Finland. His ascent has set a new benchmark in bouldering and left the climbing community in awe.

"Every move was a test of endurance and precision," Bosi said after completing the route. "This send proves that perseverance truly pays off."
