Climbing Terminology

Climbing has a rich vocabulary that varies by region. Here are a few key terms:

Crux
The hardest move or sequence on a climb.
Beta
Climbing information or advice about a route, often shared by other climbers.
Rotpunkt
A German term meaning "redpoint," referring to climbing a route cleanly from the ground without falls after practicing it.

Epic Climbing Destinations

El Capitan

The iconic 3,000-foot granite monolith that defines big wall climbing

Yosemite National Park, California, USA

Ceüse

A limestone paradise featuring some of the most aesthetic and technical sport routes in the world

Near Gap, Hautes-Alpes, France

Kalymnos

A sport climbing paradise featuring tufas, stalactites, and overhanging caves on perfect limestone

Kalymnos Island, Greece

Siurana

Historic climbing area with crimpy, technical routes on red and orange conglomerate rock

Catalonia, Spain

The Grampians

A bouldering and traditional climbing mecca with unique sandstone formations

Victoria, Australia

Rocklands

A world-class bouldering destination with thousands of problems on vibrant orange and red sandstone

Cederberg, South Africa
